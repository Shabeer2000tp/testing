Features That Power SprintSync

Interactive Scrum Board

Visualize your project workflow with dynamic drag-and-drop task cards that make tracking progress seamless and intuitive.

Global Calendar Integration

Sync your sprint timelines, deadlines, and team meetings in one centralized calendar to avoid overlaps and missed milestones.

Automated Notifications

Never miss a deadline—SprintSync keeps your team on track with smart reminders for task updates, sprint reviews, and retrospectives.

Progress Analytics Dashboard

Gain insights into team performance using burndown charts, velocity metrics, and completion rates to make data-driven decisions.

Role-Based Access

Organize your team efficiently with roles such as Product Owner, Scrum Master, and Developer—each with customized access to relevant modules.

Collaborative Documentation

Centralize all sprint-related notes, documents, and reports in one shared space accessible by all team members.
